Online Restaurent Management System

Online restaurant management system is a web application in which the customer can place his/her order based on their interests in advance by giving their details and can be able to book preferred dinning table also. This application helps to reduce the waiting time of customer at restaurant for food. Customer can also cancel their order by giving the details which was given at the booking time.
